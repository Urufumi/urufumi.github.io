About

I am currently a 4th-year Math-Computer Science major at University of California San Diego. When I was kid, I wanted to become an artist for a living. That dream has since changed, but I still constantly yearn for moments in which I can express my creativity. I am seeking a career where I can combine both my technical and creative skills to help create something that I can be proud of.

In terms of extracurriculars, I am an officer of UCSD's Anime Club: Cal Animage Beta. I am in charge of maintaining the website and drawing any art assets the club may need for promotional material. I am also a part of a competitive urban dance team on campus, Sleepless Collective (link to video of our most recent competition). When not handling my officer duties or dancing, I enjoy playing video games and watching movies.
